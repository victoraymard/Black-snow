How We Started

Passionate about the mountains and winter sports, it is through the initiative of 3 lifelong friends: Eloi, Arthur and Victor that Black Snow was born in 2020 with the desire to offer simple, effective and efficient ranges.

Based since the beginning in Saint-Lary Soulan, it is in the heart of the Pyrenees that we continue to develop our products, as close as possible to the riders.

Our spirit

We wanted a name that would have an impact and be easy to remember. Indeed, thinking of black snow seems to be illogical, so it was with this oxymoron that we wanted to name our company. We also wanted to be able to propose new sober bases. Black is also the timeless colour of sobriety.

French quality

All our products are handmade in France, with materials also of French origin.

Built to last

After a training with the greatest shapers, we assure you an unbeatable quality on all our products.

Customer Support

Because we are very sensitive to what our clients think, and it's bad enough to have problems. We guarantee an impeccable after-sales service.
